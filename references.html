Prof. Dr. Pietro Bonizzi (P.)

During my time at Maastricht University, I had the opportunity to work closely with Professor Bonizzi, who supervised my thesis. Together, we developed a state-of-the-art approach utilizing an image upscaling model from the generative AI domain to denoise ECG signals. Our approach outperformed traditional signal processing methods such as digital filters.

I highly recommend reaching out to Professor Bonizzi for feedback on my work ethic and performance, as he has suggested. He will be able to provide the most insightful information regarding our collaboration and the project we developed.
